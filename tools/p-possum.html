Portsmouth Physiological and Operative Severity Score
生理評分 Physiological Score
原始 POSSUM 以血中尿素 Urea (mmol/L) 計分，此處已換算為 BUN (mg/dL)：<21 / 21–28 / 29–42 / >42 對應 urea <7.5 / 7.5–10 / 10–15 / >15 mmol/L（BUN = urea × 2.8）。
手術嚴重度評分 Operative Severity Score
分級參考範例 Grading guide / examples
Minor: hernia repair, appendectomy, minor skin/soft tissue procedures, minor limb amputation
Moderate: cholecystectomy, hysterectomy, segmental colectomy, hemorrhoidectomy
Major: total gastrectomy, hepatic resection, elective aortic aneurysm repair, major joint replacement
Major+: pancreaticoduodenectomy (Whipple), esophagectomy, ruptured aortic aneurysm repair, cardiac surgery

此為統計預測工具，用於術前風險評估與族群比較（如手術品質稽核），並非個別病人絕對死亡機率。
